Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?
Visit our admissions page to start your application online. You'll need to upload required documents including your academic transcripts, identification, and a personal statement. Our admissions team will review your application and contact you within 5 business days.
How can I pay my fees?
You can pay fees through our online portal using mobile money or bank transfer. Alternatively, visit the finance office for bank transfer details. We accept payments in installments with an approved payment plan.
What programs do you offer?
We offer a wide range of programs in business, technology, healthcare, and creative arts. Each program combines practical skills with theoretical knowledge. Visit our programs page for detailed information on course offerings and durations.
Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?
Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Applications open twice a year. Contact our finance office at finance@ghschools.online for eligibility criteria and application deadlines.
