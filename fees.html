Admission & School Fees Policy
Admission Fee

All admitted students must pay a one-time, non-refundable admission fee of GHC 800 within 14 days of receiving their admission letter to secure their slot. Student numbers are issued upon payment.

School Fees

Students must pay 80% of tuition before registration and 100% before the semester ends to receive a Full Fees Card, which is required for end-of-semester exams. No card = No exam.

Flexible Payment

Students with genuine financial challenges may request the Flex-Digital-Accounts installment plan by contacting the finance office directly.
