Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions and enrollment.

Where can I get the GH Schools application e-voucher?
You can visit any nearby bank office to purchase our application e-voucher. However, our online portal now accepts direct payment — no e-voucher needed!
How much does the application fee cost?
The application fee is GHC 150, paid securely through our online portal. No additional e-voucher is required.
Can I revisit my application after submitting it online?
Yes! You can revisit and edit your application before the deadline. Simply log back into the portal with your credentials to make any changes.
What do I do if I have difficulty logging in?
Contact our support team or visit the admissions office directly for assistance. You can also reach us on WhatsApp at +233 539 816 189.
If I have D7 in English and Mathematics, can I still be admitted?
You may be considered for a Diploma program or may be required to retake WASSCE. We encourage you to apply and our admissions team will assess your eligibility personally.
