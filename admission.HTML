Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions and enrollment at GH Schools.

Where can I get the GH Schools application e-voucher?
Great news — you don't need an e-voucher! GH Schools now accepts direct payment through our secure online portal. Simply apply, pay GHC 200 online, and you're done.
How much does the application fee cost?
The application fee is GHC 200, paid securely through our online portal. No additional e-voucher or bank visit is required.
Can I revisit my application after submitting it online?
Yes! You can revisit and edit your application before the deadline. Simply log back into the portal with your credentials to make any changes you need.
What do I do if I have difficulty logging in?
Contact our support team or visit the admissions office directly. You can also reach us on WhatsApp at +233 54 462 2250 for quick assistance.
If I have D7 in English or Mathematics, can I still be admitted?
You may still be considered for our ICC or Diploma programs, or may be required to retake WASSCE. We encourage you to apply — our admissions team will personally assess your eligibility.
What programs does GH Schools offer?
GH Schools offers the 1-Year ICC Certificate and 2-Year CTVET Diploma across 5 schools: Media, Fashion, Catering, Cosmetology, and Technology. Each program is hands-on and career-focused.
