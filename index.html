GH Schools is a private technical and vocational institution in Achimota, Accra,Ghana. We provide hands-on, competency-based training. It is approved by the Ghana Education Service (GES) and the Commission for Technical and Vocational Education and Training (CTVET)..

GH Media School is a specialised media training institution in Ghana focused on practical, competency-based education in journalism and creative arts

GH Cosmetology School is a beauty training institution in Accra, Ghana, established in 2018 as part of the GH Schools brand
