Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions & Enrollment
Do I need prior fashion experience?

No. Our Foundation Craft courses are designed for absolute beginners with no prior background.

What is the age requirement?

Our short courses are open to everyone aged 16 and above, including busy professionals and retirees.

Course Details & Sessions
Are the materials provided?

Standard training equipment is provided on campus, but students are required to buy their personal basic tools (e.g., measuring tapes, scissors).

What is the "After Church" session?

Sunday practice sessions usually start from 1:30 PM to allow students to attend morning church services.

Payments & Certification
Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We allow a flexible payment plan: 60% upon admission and 40% halfway through the course.

Is the certificate accredited?

Yes, you receive a Certificate of Competence from GH Schools, which is widely recognized in the industry.

More Questions

Are there weekend classes?

Yes, all our short courses are weekend-based to accommodate workers and students.

Is there hostel accommodation?

Yes, we have hostel facilities available for students coming from far locations.

Can I switch courses after registering?

Switching courses is allowed within the first two weeks of admission, subject to availability.

Do you offer job placement?

While we don't guarantee jobs, we provide career guidance and connect students with industry opportunities.

What documents do I need for registration?

You will need a valid ID, passport-sized photographs, and a filled admission form.
